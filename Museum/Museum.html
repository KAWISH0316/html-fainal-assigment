MUSEUM 1997

Pakistan Maritime Museum (Urdu: پاک بحریہ عجائب گھر) is a maritime museum and park situated near PNS Karsaz on Habib Ibrahim Rehmatullah Road (Karsaz Road) in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan.
Naval equipment and machinery, maritime heritage and vessels. The main museum building is located inside the park of 28 acres.
