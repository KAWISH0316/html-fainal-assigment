SCIENCE GALLERY The last gallery at the Pakistan Maritime Museum is dedicated to scientific learnings and discoveries, with working models of various appliances. It is a great place to bring the kids due to the interactive displays.

WHALE SKELETON A mammoth Balaenoptera whale skeleton has been suspended from the ceiling in vicinity of Marine Life Gallery providing the visitors yet another spectacular sight. This whale had, washed-up on the shores of Karachi more than a decade ago and was retrieved by local fishermen. Later, it was treated and preserved for display at this museum. The whale is about 45 feet in length and is occasionally found in tropical waters.
